Our Facilities

Dear Students, We offer these following services.

Fully Air-Conditioned Room

Enjoy a cool and comfortable reading and working environment with our fully air-conditioned rooms, perfect for long study sessions or leisurely reading.

WiFi Facility

Access high-speed internet throughout the library. Whether you're conducting research or simply browsing, our reliable WiFi ensures you stay connected.

RO Water Facility

Stay hydrated with our clean and safe RO water facility, available for all Studens and visitors.

Charging Boards with Light at Every Seat

Each seat is equipped with a charging board and light, allowing you to charge your devices and have adequate lighting for reading or working.

Comfortable Chairs

Our ergonomic and comfortable chairs are designed to provide maximum comfort, so you can focus on your study and work without any discomfort.

Cafeteria

Take a break and grab a bite at our on-site cafeteria, offering a variety of snacks and beverages to keep you refreshed.
